## How to Use

1. Open the URLs above on your computer
2. Show them to your phone camera when prompted
3. Or take screenshots and use the UPLOAD FILE feature

## Tips

- Good lighting is essential
- Hold phone 6-12 inches from QR code
- Make sure the entire QR code is visible
- Try different angles if it doesn't work immediately
