# Privacy Policy for Terminal Veil

**Effective Date:** February 9, 2024

**Developer:** Joshua Fischl

## Overview

Terminal Veil is a mixed-reality puzzle game that uses your device's camera to scan colors, shapes, and QR codes. We take your privacy seriously.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **Game Progress:** Save data (current level, inventory) stored locally on your device
- **Camera Access:** Used only during active scanning - images are processed in real-time and never stored or transmitted

### What We DON'T Collect
- No personal information (name, email, location)
- No camera images or photos are stored
- No analytics or tracking
- No internet transmission of game data (web version stores sessions temporarily in server memory only)
- No third-party data sharing

## Camera Usage

The camera is used solely for gameplay:
- Colors, shapes, and QR codes detected in real-time
- All processing happens locally on your device
- No images are saved, uploaded, or shared
- Camera access requires your explicit permission

## Data Storage

### iOS/Android Native App
- Save data stored in app's local storage
- Deleted when app is uninstalled

### Web Edition
- Game sessions stored temporarily in server memory
- Sessions auto-expire after inactivity
- No persistent storage of player data

## Third-Party Services

This app does not use:
- Analytics services (Google Analytics, etc.)
- Advertising networks
- Social media SDKs
- Cloud storage services
