### Full Description (Copy ALL of this)
```
**The Veil hides the truth. Your camera is the key.**

Terminal Veil is a mixed-reality puzzle adventure where you use your device's camera to interact with the game. Scan colors, shapes, QR codes, and barcodes from the real world to solve puzzles and progress through 8 challenging sectors.

## How to Play
1. Open the game on your phone or computer
2. Type commands in the terminal or tap SCAN
3. Point your camera at real objects:
   - **Red objects** for the Crimson Gate
   - **QR codes** with "VEIL-42" for Encoded Transmission
   - **Circles** for Geometric Lock
   - And more...

## Features
- 8 unique puzzle sectors
- Real-time camera scanning (colors, shapes, QR, barcodes)
- Cyberpunk terminal aesthetic
- Save/load progress
- Works on phone, tablet, or desktop
- No download required - play in browser!

## Platforms
**Web:** Works on any device with a camera (iPhone, Android, Desktop)
**iOS:** Native app coming soon

## Tips
- Use Safari on iOS for best camera support
- Good lighting helps with color/shape detection
- Hold QR codes 6-12 inches from camera
- Type "help" for commands

---

Made with Python, Flask, and OpenCV
MIT License
```

## Step 7: Upload Game Files

Since this is an HTML game that needs a server:

### Option A: Host elsewhere (Recommended)
1. Deploy to PythonAnywhere, Render, or Heroku
2. Set "Kind of project" to "HTML"
3. Add link in description: `Play at: [your-url]`

### Option B: Embed (if hosted)
1. Use "Embed" option
2. Enter your game URL
